**Implication for the project:** The spin-averaged description (where $\langle S_{\mu\nu}\rangle = 0$ but $s^2 > 0$) is the correct framework for cosmological models, avoiding the Böhmer-Bronowski incompatibility.

1. **Hehl & Datta 1971 (4.8):** Dirac spin is totally antisymmetric: $\tau_{\alpha\beta\gamma} = -(\hbar c/4)\bar\psi\gamma_{[\alpha}\gamma_\beta\gamma_{\gamma]}\psi$
